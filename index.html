About me

I received the B.S. degree from Tianjin College, University of Science and Technology Beijing and the M.S. degree from University of Science and Technology Beijing. I was a Visiting Ph.D. Student (Co-advisor: Professor Alessandro Giua) in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at University of Cagliari (under the grant of China Scholarship Council (CSC)). I am currently working toward my Ph.D. degree in control theory and control engineering with Xidian University.
